SMART MEDICAL DISPENSER

our idea is to setup a smart medical dispenser in which doctor examines the patient through video conferencing. Further the prescribed medicines from the doctor will be automatically dispensed in the dispenser.


IDEA

We deploy the Anytime Medical Counter in all the rural areas where people cannot get the doctor on track. We install Heart Beat,Temperature sensor, Ultrasonic sensor, pressure sensor, weighing machine, Camera and Head phones to the medical dispenser. An Application is installed in both the ends for audio and visual communication & chatting with doctor.Smart medical dispenser is attached which delivers the medicines prescribed by the doctor.


PROBLEMS

to improve medical adherence, various methods are being carried out. But the problem with the existing system is, it has only dispensers that dispense medicines according to schedule. Also the people in rural areas may not be aware of those applications for medical support.


UNIQUE SELLING POINT

*Video conferencing

This video conferencing technique bridge the gap between doctor and patient.

*Medical dispenser.

The patients get the medicines instantly through medical dispenser.

SOLUTIONS
